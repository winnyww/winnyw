Flower Timer

Flower Timer

This is an installation project that links human physical
interaction with an animation of flowers in different conditions.


The flower is a metaphor of the connection between the two participants, which will change when the participants engage in physical contact
such as holding hand.

When the two participants aren't engaging in physical interaction,
the grey flower animation indicates the social distance;
when the particpants start to interact, holding hands, touching, the flower animation will become brighter and delighful, indicating a close and warm social relationship.

I wish this project could bring amazing moments
and also inspire people to think of their everyday life and their social distance among their loved ones.
