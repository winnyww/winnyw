Hey! Where's your phone

Hey! Where's your phone-prototype process

This is a project of wireless wearable fashion that expresses a message, in a fashion way.


Almost everyone finds themselves spend a good amount of time looking for their phone. Every now and then, we will need a reminder for ourselves: hey! where is your phone? Drawing inspiration from such experiences, I decided to address this piece of repeated reminder in an artistic and playful way--to put it on a t-shirt. Phone is an inseparable part of life; the urgent need to keep our phones around all the time has already been the fashion of our time. So why not wear it on us?

However, the school was closed because of COVID-19 and I had no access to many resources, the process of applying thermoChromic ink to garment wasn’t easy. A better option would be screen printing, but my only option was to make a mode with cardboard and paint with brushes. So to some degree, the fabrication result wasn’t as satisfying but was the best I could do.

I will finish this product when I'm back in campus studio with the materials/tools I need.
